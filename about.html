About Twin Paradise Apartments

Established in August 2021, Twin Paradise has grown into a joyful and secure community, marking nearly 4 years of harmonious living in the heart of the city.

Our Structure

The apartment complex consists of two blocks: 47th Block and 48th Block. Each block features:

  • 13 floors
  • 10 houses on each floor
  • Totaling 260 homes per block

Community Celebrations

At Twin Paradise, we value culture, unity, and festivity. Every year, we come together to celebrate:

New Year Pongal Republic Day Tamil New Year Ramzan Independence Day Diwali Christmas

Our festivals are filled with lights, laughter, food, and shared memories among all residents.
